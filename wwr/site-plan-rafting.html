Overview

Purpose

The purpose of our White Water Rafting site is to introduce our on-line customers and future customers to our venue, services and activities. We hope to increase our customer base by providing information on up-coming activities, discounts and training.

Audience

The target audience for our White Water Rafting website are people of all ages and skill levels who wish to learn more about rafting, our venue and our other activies and training. Our audience will be people who in live in the Salmon River area of Idaho or those who plan to vacation in the area. We have customers from 18 months old to 98 years old.
